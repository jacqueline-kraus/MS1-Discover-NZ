Finding Accommodations

General Tips

  • Be open for shared airbnbs! If you want to get to know the country and its people, that's the best way!
  • Check your options before you start your trip.
  • I highly recommend a rental car in NZ. If this is not an option for you, take into consideration, that you have to be able to reach your accommodation with other transports.
  • Depending on your travel buddy and your preference, there is also an option to rent a van and sleep on campingsides. Wild camping is forbidden in NZ!
