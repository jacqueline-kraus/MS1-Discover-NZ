Highlights of New Zealand

Top 10 Must Sees in New Zealand


Here is a list of MUST SEES that you can easily include in your trip and which are really worth visiting!

  1. Hobbiton
  2. Tongariro
  3. Milford Sound
  4. Doubtful Sound
  5. Queenstown
  6. Franz-Josef Glacier
  7. Hokitika
  8. Wellington
  9. Rotorua
  10. Aucklandmuseum

1. Hobbiton

Visit the shire! See the movie set of the Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit!
Price per adult: NZ$89.00 (ca. 53€) for a 2h Movie Set Tour
Address: 501 Buckland Road, Matamata 3472
Link to Hobbiton

2. Tongariro

Enjoy an almost 20km hike over volcanoes and stunning nature!
Price: Free (eventually cost for a shuttle bus)
Address: Tongariro National Park, Manawatu-Wanganui 4691
Link to Tongariro

3. Milford Sound

Amazing Fjords and a boat tour to the open sea!
Price per adult: Boat cruise from NZ$59 (ca 35€)
Address: Fjordland National Park
Link to Milford Sound

4. Doubtful Sound

Another stunning Fjord, yet totally different and not so easy to reach!
Price per adult: Boat cruise from NZ$179 (ca 107€)
Address: Fjordland National Park
Link to Doubtful Sound

5. Queenstown

Cute city, nice hiking tracks and a lot of nature. From here you can visit a lot of attractions of the south of New Zealand!
Price /
Address: Queenstown, New Zealand
Link to Queenstown

6. Franz-Josef Glacier

Have you ever been on a glacier? Here’s your chance! Hike or fly? Depends on your budget!
Price: Walk from NZ$82 (ca 49€), Heli Hike from NZ$485 (ca 290€)
Address: West Coast 7886, New Zealand
Link to Franz-Josef Glacier

7. Hokitika

See the most beautiful sunset at the beach of Hokitika.
Price: /
Address: Hokitika New Zealand
Link to Hokitika

8. Wellington

New Zealands capital city is definitely worth a visit!
Price: /
Address: Wellington, New Zealand
Link to Wellington

9. Rotorua

The geysers in Rotorua are incredibly fascinating! But be prepared, you will smell the sulfur!
Price: /
Address: Rotorua, New Zealand
Link to Rotorua

10. Aucklandmuseum

If you want to see the biggest city in New Zealand, the visit of this museum is a must!
Price per adult: NZ$28 (ca 17€)
Address: Parnell, Auckland 1010, New Zealand
Link to Aucklandmuseum
